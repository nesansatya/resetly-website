Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 25 March 2026  ·  Version 1.0  ·  Governed by PDPA 2010 (Malaysia)

1. Introduction

Weekly Reset ("the App") is operated by Resetly ("we", "our", "us"), accessible at resetly.my. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal data in accordance with the Personal Data Protection Act 2010 (PDPA) of Malaysia.

By using the App, you consent to the collection and use of your data as described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the App immediately.

2. Data We Collect

We collect the following categories of personal data:

  • Account data: Full name, email address, and password (stored securely via Supabase Auth)
  • Health and wellness data: Body weight, health goals, daily mood and energy levels, exercise completion records, habit logs, and streak data
  • Usage data: Login timestamps, feature usage, and app interactions
  • Payment data: Subscription status and billing information — processed and stored securely by Stripe. We do not store your card details on our servers

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and personalise the App's wellness features
  • To calculate your personalised hydration goal and workout recommendations
  • To track your progress, streaks, and habit consistency
  • To process your Pro subscription payments via Stripe
  • To communicate service updates, account information, and support responses
  • To improve the App based on usage patterns (aggregated, anonymised data only)

4. Legal Basis for Processing

  • Consent: You provide explicit consent upon registration
  • Contractual necessity: Processing is required to deliver the services you have subscribed to
  • Legitimate interests: To maintain App security, prevent fraud, and improve our services

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We share your data only with:

  • Supabase (database and authentication provider) — for secure data storage
  • Stripe (payment processor) — for subscription billing
  • Vercel (hosting provider) — for App delivery

We may disclose your data if required by Malaysian law, court order, or governmental authority.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your personal data will be permanently deleted within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

7. Your Rights Under PDPA 2010

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete personal data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing
  • Request deletion of your personal data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at [email protected]. We will respond within 21 days.

8. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data, including encrypted connections (HTTPS), row-level security in our database, and secure authentication via JWT tokens.

9. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for individuals under 18 years of age. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal data, contact us immediately at [email protected].

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or in-app notification.
