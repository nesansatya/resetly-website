How it works

Open it. Do the thing.
Close it. Feel better.

1
Sign up in 30 seconds

Email, password, and one question: what's your goal? More energy, better sleep, lose weight, or build habits. That's it — no onboarding maze.

2
Check your Today dashboard

Every morning, your dashboard shows the workout, your habits, water goal, and a mood check-in. Everything is in one place.

3
Log how you feel

Tap your mood and energy level. The app adapts its recommendations instantly — pushing harder when you're energised, pulling back when you're not.

4
Watch the patterns emerge

After a week, your summary shows what you completed. After a month, you'll see which habits moved the needle. The data is yours, and it tells a story.
